When you connect your social media accounts to our service, we collect:

  • Your email address (if provided during authentication)
  • Social media account information (username, profile data)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide social media management services
  • Post content to your connected social media accounts
  • Monitor and display your social media activity

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your social media tokens are stored securely and used only for authorized actions.

Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with social media platforms including X (Twitter), Facebook, and Instagram. These platforms have their own privacy policies that govern how they handle your data.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time.
